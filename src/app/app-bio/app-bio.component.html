What are we?

HipHost connects travelers to locals based on a unique matching algorithm called VelocitiTM. Our formula combines mutual demographics, personality traits, interests & activities to help a traveler meet their perfect local counterpart - like when a friend comes to visit!

Travelers on HipHost can chat, get valuable insight & meet up with hosts that help enrich their travel experience. In turn, using a simple review system, Hosts benefit by earning “Connect Coins”, which they get rewarded with for giving great advice or meeting up with like-minded individuals & showing them the pearls of the city! “Connect Coins” can be converted into cash and deposited into the Host’s Paypal account.

This system (we believe) will entice both locals & travelers to connect with one another! The founding members of HipHost believe that the best moments abroad or in a new city are when like-minded people (from different parts of the world) come together to interact, share stories & create lifelong bonds in a short space of time.

Our mission is to foster these connections, ensuring travelers don’t miss out on unforgettable memories all because some guys with a great idea were too lazy to bring this app to life! Now go out, get lost in the city and enjoy your new found travelling freedom...
