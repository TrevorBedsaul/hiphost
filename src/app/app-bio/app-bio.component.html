What are we?

HipHost connects travelers to locals based on a unique matching algorithm called VelocitiTM. Our formula combines mutual demographics, interests & activities to help you meet your perfect local counterpart. Like when a friend comes to visit! Travelers on HipHost have the ability to chat, get valuable insight and potentially meet up with hosts that will enrich their experience in a given city. In turn, Hosts benefit by earning “Connect Coins” based on a simple review system. These “Connect Coins” are converted into cash and deposited to the Host’s Paypal account.

This system we believe will entice both locals and travelers to connect with one another! From personal experience we have found that the best moments abroad or in a new city are when like-minded people from different parts of the world meet one another. Our mission is to foster these connections that so many people miss out on when they go to a new place.
