Safety

Last Updated: 13 July 2018

It is expected that HipHost members are honest and aware of their responsibilities both as travelers and hosts. However, you should never forget basic safety precautions when meeting a person for the first time.

• If you arrange a meeting with another member, be sure to contact them by phone or webcam beforehand to make sure you are meeting with the same person you’ve been contacting.

• For your own safety, inform a close friend or relative about your plans and whether you are meeting as a host or a traveler.

Please note that HipHost does not take any responsibility for the safety of their members while they are hanging out. This website is only a venue for members to chat and set up meetings together.
